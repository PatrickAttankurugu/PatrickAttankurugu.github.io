About Me

Hi, I'm Patrick Attankurugu

I'm Patrick Attankurugu, an AI developer and Entrepreneur with a keen focus on leveraging AI to solve some of Africa's most pressing challenges. I have a background in Computer Science from the University of Ghana.
Over the course of 2022 and 2023, I went through two business incubation programs, one at Kosmos Innovation Center where I co-founded a startup called PIGCASS-focused on a symbiotic relationship between cassava farming and pig rearing and aimed at export, the other at Ghana Digital Centres Limited at the Accra Digital Centre, where I co-founded a startup called SEMA-focussed on using AI to proactively control crime.

I am the CEO of SEMA Technologies, where we steer the frontier of AI to meet Africa's unique challenges and opportunities.
My vision extends into philanthropy as the President and co-founder of Restored Glory Foundation, where we champion the sustainable development of orphans and vulnerable children in Ghana, as it is written, What God the Father considers to be pure and genuine religion is this: to take care of orphans and widows in their suffering and to keep oneself from being corrupted by the world.
Through the foundation, we hope to extend God's hand of love to all who need it, without any biases towards religion or culture. With each endeavor, from technology to humanitarian efforts, my aim is to forge pathways to empowerment and lasting change.

Aside working on my company and non-profit foundation, I spend my time developing websites for individuals and organizations alike.
I've completed several online and in person courses on career growth, CV design and Linkedin Profile optimization, This positions me well to craft compelling Resumes, and Linkedin profiles for job seekers. I also guide job seekers to enroll in appropriate courses to develop job ready skills.

Join me in this journey towards a brighter, AI-driven tomorrow.
