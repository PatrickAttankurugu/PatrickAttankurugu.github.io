About Me

Hi, I'm Patrick Attankurugu

I'm Patrick Attankurugu, an AI developer with a keen focus on leveraging technology for impactful solutions. My journey has been enriched by participating in incubators like the Kosmos Innovation Centre AgriTech Challenge and the Tertiary Digital Innovation Program, highlighting my commitment to innovation in tech and agriculture. Currently, I'm broadening my entrepreneurial skills through the Jim Leech Mastercard Foundation Fellowship at Queen's University. This blend of AI expertise and business acumen drives my mission to develop technology that not only advances industries but also addresses societal challenges.

I lead a technology company called sematechnologies.com as CEO, steering the frontier of AI to meet Africa's unique challenges and opportunities. My vision extends into philanthropy as the founder and President of The Restored Glory Foundation, where we champion the sustainable development of orphans in Ghana. With each endeavor, from technology to humanitarian efforts, my aim is to forge pathways to empowerment and lasting change.

Join me in this journey towards a brighter, technology-driven tomorrow.
